How we built the list

HVAC contractors aren't on LinkedIn. They're on Google Maps at 7am and in a truck by 8. So we started there.

81,118 HVAC contractors pulled from Google Maps listings across the US. Filtered to owners and GMs at shops with 5-50+ trucks.
Personal emails from their own websites. These owners put their email in the footer - jason@julianheatandair.com, not info@company.com.
No AI, no smart, no digital transformation in the copy. These are contractors who've been burned by tech that overpromises. The language had to sound like someone who's sat across from them, not above them.
Subject line: "maintenance plans." Not "Scale your membership program" or "Grow recurring revenue."
CTA was a 20-minute ROI review, not a demo. Contractors schedule appointments all day. "We'll run your numbers" is how they think. "Book a demo" isn't.
What a real campaign would add

This was a proof of concept with no access to SmartAC's team, CRM, or existing pipeline. Every contractor got the same email with a first name swap.

With input on which markets to hit, what's landing on sales calls, and which contractors are already in your pipeline to exclude - the targeting gets tighter and there's follow-up instead of one email.
